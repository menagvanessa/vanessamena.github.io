43K+ followers
I kept noticing that women's voices in Latin music weren't getting the space they deserved. Instead of waiting for someone else to fix it, I built something. Música con M de Mujer is a Spanish-language podcast amplifying female artists — from indie newcomers to barrier-breaking names. I designed the format, hosted and produced every episode, and grew a community of 43K+ across TikTok and Instagram through intentional storytelling, platform-specific content, and targeted promotion. Not just a passion project — a movement built around visibility, authenticity, and impact.
Seguía notando que las voces femeninas en la música latina no recibían el espacio que merecían. En lugar de esperar, construí algo. Música con M de Mujer amplifica a las artistas femeninas. Diseñé el formato, conduje y produje cada episodio, y construí una comunidad de más de 43K en TikTok e Instagram mediante storytelling intencional. No es solo un proyecto — es un movimiento de visibilidad, autenticidad e impacto.
